Many people in this app are using the app to record illness symptoms, taking medicine

Since it is a time recording application, there are users who use it in various ways.

  • A record of when you took your medicine
  • Records of illness symptoms

...etc. Many people are using the app to record for. Here are some tips on how to use it.

To reflect on own actions

It is the first thing to take a record such as "drinking medicine at a specified time",
but by keeping a daily activity record, "behaving badly for your illness" May be noticed.
(For example, sleep time may be delayed)

Moreover, if you go one step further, you will have the opportunity to think for yourself, "Why do you take that action?"
(Example: I have a habit of doing a small job just before going to bed)
From there, you can fix your bad habits little by little.

At the doctor's examination...

When you see a doctor, you will be asked about your medical condition.
But It is difficult immediately on the spot to convey the details as...

  • illness symptoms
  • when you took medicine

Therefore, it would be even better if you could keep a record and, if possible, take a quick look before the medical examination and summarize what you want to tell the doctor.
It's hard to tell in detail what happened in the past.

Also, when the doctor in charge changes, it's easy to tell my situation because I have the records from a long time ago.

Other good things to record

A stable lifestyle that is not related to your immediate illness is less likely to get sick,
and will help you recover if you do.
Therefore, it is good to keep the following life records and improve them if your lifestyle is disturbed.

  • Meal records
  • Exercise record
  • Bedtime record

You can objectively review your life with the records you made with the app, and you can fix bad habits yourself.
Try recording your activity once in the app.
